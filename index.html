FREQUENT QUESTIONS

How much should I spend on ads?

It depends on your business goals. We usually recommend starting with a small daily budget and increasing it once we see good results.

Do you guarantee results?

We guarantee professional management and high-quality ads. While no one can guarantee sales, our data-driven approach maximizes your chances of success.

Will you design the ads?

Yes, our team handles all the graphics and video editing needed for your Google and Meta campaigns.

Which is better: Google or Meta?

Google is great for people actively searching for you. Meta (Facebook/Instagram) is great for building brand awareness and finding new audiences.

How long is the contract?

We work on a month-to-month basis. We want you to stay with us because of the results we deliver, not because of a long contract.
